Our store pledges to donate 5% to 10% of our annual income every year to aid stray animals. In addition to that, we rescue injured, abused, or homeless animals and provide them with shelter and care. Furthermore, we facilitate the re-homing of abandoned animals, ensuring they find loving homes.

Every year, we hold online or offline fundraising events to appeal to the public for donations to help stray animals. In addition to monetary contributions, individuals can also donate supplies such as cat food, dog food, toys, and cleaning supplies.

Every year, we organize various educational campaigns to raise awareness about caring for animals.
